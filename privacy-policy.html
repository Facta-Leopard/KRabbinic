KRabbinic Privacy Policy

KRabbinic is an iPhone-first Korean Myeongli learning app. The current release includes structured Myeongli learning, Twelve Animal Signs study, Chart Practice, Daily Myeongli Analysis, Apple Intelligence assisted AI Tutor features, settings for saved birth profiles, and Google AdMob banner advertising.

Repository: github.com/Facta-Leopard/KRabbinic
Support issues: github.com/Facta-Leopard/KRabbinic/issues
Contact: shteosis@gmail.com

English

1. Data Stored on Your Device

  • Optional birth profile information that you choose to enter, such as birth date, birth time, calendar type, timezone, and daylight saving correction.
  • Saved chart readings and cached interpretation results.
  • App preferences such as language, appearance mode, and related settings.

2. Local Myeongli Calculation and Learning Content

KRabbinic calculates Saju charts and study references with bundled data included in the app. In the current release, the user-facing experience centers on Myeongli Learning, Twelve Animal Signs study, Chart Practice, Daily Myeongli Analysis, and AI Tutor assistance. KRabbinic does not operate its own remote server for chart calculation or educational content delivery. Birth profile data entered in the app is intended to stay local to the device unless required by an Apple system framework or a third-party advertising SDK described below.

3. Apple Intelligence

On supported devices and system configurations, KRabbinic can use Apple's system Apple Intelligence frameworks for learning assistance and interpretation-style responses. If Apple Intelligence is unavailable, disabled, not ready, or unsupported for the selected language, KRabbinic falls back to its built-in reading and learning flow based on the same calculated chart.

4. Advertising, ATT, and Consent

KRabbinic includes Google AdMob banner advertising. Depending on your region, platform state, and privacy choices, the app may use Google's User Messaging Platform (UMP) to request advertising consent and Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT) prompt to request tracking permission before loading certain ads.

Google AdMob and related SDK components may process data such as device identifiers, advertising data, product interaction data, coarse location, performance data, crash data, and other diagnostics according to Google's SDK behavior, your device settings, and your consent choices.

5. Third Parties

  • Apple system frameworks, including Apple Intelligence when available.
  • Google Mobile Ads SDK (AdMob) for banner advertising.
  • Google User Messaging Platform for consent and privacy-choice flows when required.
  • GitHub Pages for hosting this privacy policy and the support page.

6. What KRabbinic Does Not Intentionally Run

  • No KRabbinic account system.
  • No KRabbinic-operated cloud storage for birth profiles.
  • No KRabbinic-operated remote chart-calculation server in the current release.

7. Your Controls

  • You can edit or remove the saved birth profile inside the app.
  • You can clear saved readings inside the app.
  • You can revisit privacy choices inside the app when that entry point is required.
  • You can control tracking permission through iOS settings.
  • Removing the app from your device removes app-local data managed by iOS.

8. Learning and Entertainment Notice

KRabbinic is designed for cultural learning, structured Myeongli study, self-reflection, and entertainment. It does not provide professional health, legal, financial, medical, immigration, employment, or safety advice.

9. Contact

For privacy questions, support, or repository-related requests, use the GitHub issue tracker or email shteosis@gmail.com.
