English

1. Before You Contact Support

  • KRabbinic currently targets iPhone first and requires iOS 26 or later.
  • The app currently includes Myeongli Learning, Twelve Animal Signs study, Chart Practice, Daily Myeongli Analysis, AI Tutor, settings, and banner ads.
  • Apple Intelligence responses may fall back to the built-in learning flow if Apple Intelligence is unavailable, disabled, not ready, or unsupported for the selected language.
  • Banner ads may depend on consent status, ATT permission, region, and AdMob readiness.

2. What to Include in a Bug Report

  • Device model and iOS version.
  • KRabbinic app version.
  • App language.
  • Whether the issue appears in Myeongli Learning, Twelve Animal Signs study, Chart Practice, Daily Myeongli Analysis, AI Tutor, settings, or ads.
  • Steps to reproduce the problem.
  • Screenshots or screen recordings if helpful.

3. If the Issue Is About Chart Calculation

  • Calendar type used.
  • Timezone selected.
  • Whether daylight saving correction was enabled.
  • Whether birth time was known or estimated.
  • You may replace real birth data with a fictional test profile unless exact data is required.

4. If the Issue Is About Ads or Privacy Choices

  • Your region or App Store country if relevant.
  • Whether the ATT prompt appeared.
  • Whether the privacy-choice form appeared.
  • Whether the app showed a placeholder banner or a loaded ad.

5. Sensitive Data

Do not post private personal information in a public GitHub issue. If an exact birth profile is not required, use a partial, approximate, or fictional example.

6. Scope

KRabbinic is a learning and interpretation-support app for Korean Myeongli. It does not provide professional health, legal, financial, medical, immigration, employment, or safety advice.

7. Where to Ask for Help

For bugs and feature requests, use the GitHub issue tracker. For privacy, sponsorship, or direct support questions, email shteosis@gmail.com.
